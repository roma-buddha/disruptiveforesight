Course Briefing

Financial innovation interpreted as system change.

This course is designed to help students understand digital finance not as a list of isolated technologies, but as a shift in how trust, exchange, intermediation, and regulation are organized. The platform provides a coherent way to move through the major themes and return to them as classroom discussion evolves.

Coverage

Core themes

Fintech, digital payments, open banking, digital lending, insurtech, wealthtech, regtech, cryptoassets, tokenization, and central bank digital currencies.

Use Case

How students should use it

Read before seminars, return during discussion, and use the course as an anchor for assignments, comparative analysis, and exam preparation.
