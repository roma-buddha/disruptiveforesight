Course Overview

About the course

Financial innovation in one structured course space

This course introduces the major ways digital technologies are transforming the financial sector. It is designed as an online book, so students can move through topics step by step and return to concepts, examples, and materials whenever needed.

Key topics

Fintech, digital payments, open banking, digital lending, insurtech, wealthtech, regtech, cryptoassets, tokenization, and central bank digital currencies.

How students can use it

Use the course before class, during discussion, and when preparing assignments or exams. New cases, examples, and materials can be added over time.
