Disruptive Foresight

Interactive course books for digital change

Disruptive Foresight is a teaching platform that hosts interactive online course books on how digital technologies reshape organizations, finance, and society.

Created by Valery Chistov, Assistant Professor at Deusto Business School, University of Deusto, the platform brings course materials, concepts, examples, and readings into one structured digital space.

What Is Disruptive Foresight?

Strategic foresight meets disruptive change

Disruptive Foresight combines strategic foresight with disruptive innovation. It helps students understand how technologies, markets, institutions, and social trends reshape industries, organizations, and finance.

  • Future trends and weak signals
  • Disruptive technologies and business models
  • Industry change and strategic response

How to use the platform

Start with the course overview, then move through chapters and subsections. Use the platform before class, during class activities, and when preparing assignments or exams.

The content may evolve during the course as new examples, cases, and materials are added.
